A number of research groups and individuals have been involved in ODT, LEM, and HiPS research. A few of these include:

  • Computational Turbulent Reacting Flow Research Lab at Brigham Young University led by David Lignell.

  • Numerical Fluids and Gasdynamics Lab led by Heiko Schmidt at Brandenburg University of Technology at Cottbus-Senftenberg.

  • Oevermann Research Lab led by Michael Oevermann at Brandenburg University of Technology at Cottbus-Senftenberg.

  • Computational Combustion Lab at Georgia Tech. led by Suresh Menon.

  • Sutherland research group at the University of Utah led by James Sutherland.

  • Krueger research group at the University of Utah led by Steven K. Krueger.

  • Freire research group led by Livia Freire at the University of Sao Paulo, Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics.

  • Wu research group led by Yuxin Wu at Tsinghua University Department of Thermal Engineering.

  • Gore research group led by Jay Gore at Purdue University.
